## What is English Breakfast?

什么是英式早餐？

英式早餐是英格兰的一种传统早餐，也叫做“Fry-up”，主要以油煎的食材为主。英式早餐的传统可以追溯到中世纪。那时候人们一天通常只有两顿饭，所以为了补充体能，早餐通常由啤酒面包和一点奶酪组成。那时只有贵族才能享有一顿由多种食材和肉类组成的丰盛早餐，所以传统英早的配置相当于一种“低调的炫富方式”。贵族们准备的早餐越丰富，就代表自己的领土越广袤，由此引发了英国人对早餐的重视。

尽管叫英式早餐，但可以在任何时间吃，而且它也并不是英国人每天都吃的。更常见的是在周末或假期吃。

> English breakfast is a traditional breakfast in England, also known as "Fry-up", mainly fried food. The tradition of English breakfast can be traced back to the Middle Ages. At that time, people usually only had two meals a day, so in order to supplement their physical strength, breakfast usually consisted of beer bread and a little cheese. At that time, only the nobility could enjoy a rich breakfast consisting of a variety of ingredients and meats, so the traditional English breakfast configuration is a "low-key show-off way". The richer the breakfast prepared by the nobility, the larger the territory they represented, which sparked the British people's attention to breakfast.

一个常见的传统英式早餐通常包括培根或香肠（通常是猪肉），鸡蛋（油炸，水煮或炒），炸或烤西红柿，炸蘑菇，黑布丁，烤豆和面包，吐司和炸面包中的一种或两种。Bubble
and squeak是常见的配菜，但现在更常见的是被土豆煎饼取代。

> There is no fixed menu or set of ingredients for a full breakfast. A common traditional English breakfast typically includes back bacon or sausages (usually pork), eggs (fried, poached or scrambled), fried or grilled tomatoes, fried mushrooms, black pudding, baked beans and bread, either, or both, toast and fried bread. Bubble and squeak is a traditional accompaniment but is now more commonly replaced by hash browns.

A fried egg is a cooked dish made from one or more eggs which are removed from their shells and placed into a frying pan and fried.

- poached eggs vs soft boiled eggs

  poach eggs是不带壳在水中煮熟的，而软煮蛋是在壳中在沸水中煮熟的。

3. Toast or fried bread
   区别在于烤面包是用吐司机烤的，而炸面包是用油煎的。

一般来说：1分钟-untoasted; 2分钟-warm; 3分钟-medium toasted; 4分钟-crispy; 6分钟-done; 7分钟-burnt

> [!Caution]
> 我们店的吐司机从左到右第一格最慢，第二格最快，最后一格中等，中间两格随机。
> 使用toast的时候，用中间柔软的面包，不使用头尾的面包。
> 总是看着吐司机，不要让面包烤焦。

4. potatoes/hash browns: patties of finely chopped potatoes that have been fried until golden brown

- 土豆/薯饼：切碎的土豆饼，炸至金黄色
